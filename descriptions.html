OUR CORE BELIEF

Pak Green Foundation believes global climate change resolutions will be accelerated by individuals and communities by undertaking collective action toward solutions, which include conservation, mitigation, adaptation, policy, and innovation. We universally recognize local youth will typically inherit the unparalleled impacts of climate change and are undoubtedly among our most powerful advocates. We believe education remains a tool of empowerment, and empowering educators as critical messengers of climate literacy constitute part of the solution to climate change. We believe tree planting literacy remain the key to achieving our established goals. To prevent severe and potentially catastrophic impacts from climate change, intermediate steps to introduce more trees are necessary, lower emission transition fuels and technologies should be prioritized to obtain respiratory and heart health benefits; women and children are disproportionately affected by household air pollution.


OUR THEORY OF CHANGE

Communities collectively demand global climate change solutions. Empowering individuals in their societies to make long-term, lasting change requires building climate literacy, understanding personal connections to climate breakdown, and developing powerful advocates through a model of partnership. By engaging educators, youth, and the public, we believe communities can be better positioned to secure a resilient and equitable future.

VISSION

To universally support the next generation of climate ambassadors working flawlessly for a carbon-free environment in every community across the world.


MISSION

To plant five million Plants/ trees in the environment affected by extreme weather condition and modestly reducing pollution in our local communities by 2030.


EQUITY STATEMENTS

We understand climate change is a highly complex issue and we believe an equitable solution cannot be achieved if our focus is on climate science and policy lens alone. We must take a comprehensive perspective of climate change solution to accomplish our goal and invent our desired future. We are committed to addressing the intersection of climate change, economic, social and racial disparities by working collaboratively with partners who understand this interface.
