Chairman Message

We would like to present a tribute to Muhammad Nawaz Khan, Chairman of pak green foundation from the bottom of our hearts. This great man has been working hard and diligently for 18 years to make mirpurkhaas into an absolutely enchanting and gorgeous city. I myself have never seen a person who has such great love for nature and it's beauty. On top of his dedication to make mirpurkhaas more green he has made an absolutely gorgeous location where people come to offer Eid prayer and do various activities to celebrate the special occasion of Eid.We would like to tribute to Mr Muhammad Nawaz khan for his dedication and efforts to make a polluted area such as mirpurkhaas into a blooming, beauteous and pleasing area by requesting the Prime Minister of Islamic state of Pakistan to give him presidentship as a reward for his sincerity and hard work.


Our vission

Go Green Welfare Society (GGWS) aims at building a network within Pakistan where it can empower individuals & groups through funds and training and simultaneously promote nation building projects based on education, climate change, youth & gender empowerment and social research & advocacy.


Our Mission

Go Green Welfare Society (GGWS) is a registered society under Society Registration Act 1961. Our mission is to provide financial and strategic facilitation to individuals & groups collaborating with us on various nation building projects based on United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. Our projects focus on a) education, b) combating climate change, c) youth and gender empowerment through vocational training, skill development and creation of employment opportunities and d) social research and advocacy.
